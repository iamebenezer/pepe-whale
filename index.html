Add ETH to your wallet

Go to ‘settings’ option within your crypto wallet then locate ‘networks’ section. Click ‘add network’ then ‘add network manually’

Input the following details:

  • Network Name: ETH
  • RPC Endpoint: https://mainnet.ETH.org/
  • Chain ID: 8453
  • Currency Symbol: ETH
  • Block Explorer: https://ETHscan.org

Once you click ‘Save’, you can use the ETH network by selecting ETH from the network selection menu.

Fund your ETH wallet

For this, you have a couple of options:

  1. Transfer ETH from CoinETH, Bybit, OKX or any exchange that supports ETH network. (Must send the ETH via ETH network)

To bridge your ETH to the ETH network, connect your wallet, choose the network you wish to bridge from, select ETH as the target network then specify the amount of ETH you intend to transfer.
